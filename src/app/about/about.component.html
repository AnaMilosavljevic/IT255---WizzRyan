Our Story

WizzRyan Airways was founded in 2002 with a mission to make air travel accessible to everyone. Since then, we've grown to become one of the leading airlines in the industry, serving destinations worldwide.

Our mission is to connect people and places, fostering global connections and enriching lives through air travel. We're committed to safety, reliability, and exceptional customer service.
